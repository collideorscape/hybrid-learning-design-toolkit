HYBRID LEARNING PARTICIPANT GUIDE

👋 Welcome!

This guide helps you participate effectively in hybrid learning where some join in-person and others remotely. It balances your individual needs with our collective learning community, recognizing that we all benefit when everyone can participate meaningfully.

💫 Key Message: Effective hybrid learning requires both individual flexibility AND community presence. Together, we'll find creative ways for everyone to "show up."

🎯 During Hybrid Sessions

🎨 Strategic Presence: Balancing Individual Needs & Community Connection

📹 Camera Strategies for Community Building

The Challenge: When most cameras are off, it can:
  • Make instructors struggle to gauge understanding
  • Create disconnection between remote and on-site students
  • Reduce everyone's sense of shared learning

Your Flexible Options:

StrategyWhen to UseCommunity Impact
🟢 Full VideoWhen comfortable & possibleStrongest presence signal
📸 Key MomentsIntros, presentations, small groupsBuilds connection at crucial times
🖼️ Profile PhotoAlwaysShows you're "here"
🔄 Strategic On/OffVideo for discussion, off for lecturesBalances needs
🎭 Virtual BackgroundPrivacy with presenceProtects space, shows face
💡 Community Agreement: We each find ways to "show up" that work for our circumstances while building our learning community together.

🤝 Building Presence Without Video

When camera isn't possible, strengthen presence through:

📝 Name + Context: "Sarah here - following in chat today"
💭 Think Aloud: Share thoughts/questions regularly
🎯 Respond Quickly: To polls, questions, activities
🤗 Support Peers: Acknowledge others' contributions
⚡ Stay Active: Consistent participation signals

🌐 Creating Inclusive Community Presence

🎭 The "Presence Pact" - Our Collective Agreement

As a learning community, we agree to:

  1. Find Our Way - Each person discovers their best presence mode
  2. Show Up Somehow - Everyone signals participation regularly
  3. Respect Constraints - Honor individual circumstances
  4. Build Bridges - Help connect across modalities
  5. Communicate Openly - Share what works/doesn't work

💪 Advocacy for Inclusive Presence

🗣️ When Presence Expectations Feel Exclusionary

Your voice matters when:

  • Camera mandates ignore real constraints
  • Single-mode participation is required
  • Presence is measured narrowly
  • Community needs aren't balanced with individual needs

How to advocate:

1. 📢 Share your perspective privately first
2. 💡 Suggest creative alternatives
3. 🤝 Propose community solutions
4. 📚 Reference inclusive practices

🌈 Expanding Presence Definitions

Help your learning community recognize presence as:

  • 💬 Consistent chat participation
  • 🎯 Quality of contributions over video visibility
  • 📊 Engagement across multiple channels
  • 🔄 Flexibility in how we "show up"
  • ⏰ Sustained participation over time

🎯 Building Community Together

🤲 Your Role in Community Presence

When you CAN use camera:

  • Turn it on during introductions
  • Use it for small group work
  • Share during presentations
  • Model comfort with flexibility

When you CAN'T use camera:

  • Maximize other presence signals
  • Communicate actively
  • Support others' participation
  • Find creative alternatives
🌟 Presence Champions: Everyone can help create an inclusive learning community where diverse participation modes are valued and supported.
